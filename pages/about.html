How We Started

BUILT FOR THE
Modern Wardrobe

Urban Threads was founded in 2018 by designer and fashion industry veteran Claire Nolan. After a decade working for major European fashion houses, Claire noticed a gap in the market — a brand that could deliver genuine quality and considered design at a price point that didn't require a second mortgage.

She started Urban Threads from a small studio in Dublin with a simple premise — every piece had to be worth buying, worth wearing and worth keeping. No fast fashion filler, no trend chasing, just well made clothes designed for the real world.

Six years on, Urban Threads has grown into one of Ireland's most loved independent fashion brands with customers across Europe who keep coming back season after season.

What We Stand For

THE URBAN THREADS WAY

01

Quality Over Quantity

We would rather make fewer pieces and make them properly. Every garment is designed to be worn repeatedly, washed without fading and kept for years not months.

02

Sustainability First

Every fabric we use is sustainably sourced. We work with certified suppliers, use recycled packaging and are committed to reducing our environmental footprint season by season.

03

Designed for Real Life

Our pieces are designed to work together and work hard. Clothes that go from the office to the weekend, from the city to the coast, without missing a beat.

04

Honest Pricing

We price fairly. No inflated markdowns or fake sale prices. What you see is what it costs to make something well and we think that is worth being upfront about.
