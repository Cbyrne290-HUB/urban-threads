Quick Answers

COMMON QUESTIONS

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 2 to 3 working days. Express next day delivery is available on orders placed before 2pm. All orders are dispatched from Dublin.

Do you offer free delivery?

Yes — standard delivery is free on all orders over €75. For orders under €75 a flat rate of €4.99 applies.

What is your returns policy?

We offer free returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn with original tags attached. Simply use the returns label included in your order.

How do I find my size?

Each product page includes a detailed size guide. If you are between sizes we generally recommend sizing up. Contact us for personalised sizing advice.

Are your fabrics sustainable?

Yes — 100% of our fabrics are sustainably sourced from certified suppliers. We use organic cotton, recycled polyester and responsible linen across all our ranges.

Do you ship outside Ireland?

We currently ship to Ireland and the UK. European and international shipping is coming soon. Sign up to our newsletter to be the first to know.
